Life OR Death

Instructions to decide your Fate

  1. Click the button to initiate the game.
  2. Click texts to continue playing the game
  3. Don't Die

You wake up at a house cuffed to the bed. You somehow manage to free yourself from the cuffs. You look around and see a window, a door which is locked. You can choose to escape from the window, try to open the door or stay inside.
